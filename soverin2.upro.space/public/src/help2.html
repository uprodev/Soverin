Delete your account

Use the following steps to permantly delete your account

  1. Login to your Soverin Dashboard at my.soverin.net.
  2. Click on Account and then Settings
  3. Scroll down and click the trash can next to the “STATUS”
  4. Confirm the removal of your account by using the Remove my account
