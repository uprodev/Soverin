Features

Switching from ‘free’ to freedom has never been easier.

Same experience, different principles. After a quick and easy setup, with Soverin, you can start sending and receiving emails keeping your information private. No need to sacrifice the confidentiality of your personal data.

Calendar/contact sync.

Untraceable anonymous aliases.

Customize your email

Choose your own email address when you join.

  • Want to register or use your own domain?

    No problem. You can use your own registered domain or register one through Soverin and link it to your private email for an additional fee.

  • Do you want more good news?

    With Soverin, there is no lock-in. We know that your custom domain and email are part of your digital identity. We don’t own them, so you can take them with you whenever you want without losing your email address.

Keep using your favourite email apps

Soverin is app independent. You’re free to use it with the email apps you prefer.
